PSEUDO STYLING AND MEDIA QUERY.

METHOD 2: 
By ejecting scripts:
npm run eject

. then add the below to the webpack.config.dev.js and webpack.config.prod.js, where we have
importLoadersers under test: /\.css$/,

importLoaders: 1,
modules: true,
localIdentName: '[name]__[local]__[hash:base64:5]'
